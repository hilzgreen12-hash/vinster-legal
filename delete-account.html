Delete Your Vinster Account & Data

Vinster · Your AI Sommelier

You can delete your Vinster account and all associated data at any time. Here's how.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (instant)

  1. Open Vinster and sign in.
  2. Go to the You tab.
  3. Tap Delete Account and confirm.

Your account and data are deleted immediately and permanently — there is no undo.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you've uninstalled the app or can't sign in, email us and we'll delete your account for you:

Email: tellme@vinsterapp.com

Please email from the address on your account, with the subject "Delete my Vinster account". We action requests within 30 days (usually much sooner).

What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes all of your data, including:

  • Your account and login details
  • Your cellar, racks, and bottle-label photos
  • Your tasting notes, wine and restaurant reviews
  • Your preferences and wine/food personality sketches
  • Any reviews you shared to the community

Retention

Your data is removed from our primary database and file storage within 30 days of the request. Encrypted backups may take up to 90 days to fully expire, after which no copy remains.

For more detail on how we handle your data, see our Privacy Policy.
